Presentation

We opened our doors on Thursday, December 27, 1928, in charge of the prestigious dentist Andrés Antelo Añón, recognized for his use of innovative techniques and philanthropic spirit. Marking a milestone in the history of dental health in the country. Consolidating in the fifties and sixties as specialists in teeth.

The clinic


Since its inception the clinic has medical staff with high preparation and innovative spirit making it a specialized center in different areas of dentistry. For the health care of patients, we perform treatments based on biosafety and human quality protocols in a way that meets their needs. We seek excellence at work and our goal is success through patient satisfaction. Currently our center is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment that facilitates the diagnosis and dental treatment. We can highlight the availability of intraoral camera for the detection of caries with on-screen viewing by the patient and a digital Rx system and dental scanner that allows us to carry out implant surgery guided by a computer.
