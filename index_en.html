Services

Orthodontics

Orthodontics is a specialty of dentistry that is responsible for the study, prevention, diagnosis and treatment of anomalies in the shape, position, relationship and function of dentomaxillofacial structures. Its exercise is the art of preventing, diagnosing and correcting its possible alterations and keeping them in an optimal state of health and harmony through the use and control of different types of forces. The eagerness to correct the clenched, irregular or protruding teeth is very old because there is evidence that already in the VIII century a.C. it was trying to solve such anomalies. Among the ethnographic findings of the Greek and Etruscan world are orthodontic appliances of magnificent design.

General odontology

General dentistry are treatments aimed at solving the primary problems that have to do with the mouth, teeth and other related structures. In most cases it represents the patient's first contact with the dentist. The general dentist makes the primary diagnosis of the patient, solves the problems of restoration of caries that may present and channels it appropriately to each specialist according to the patient's need.

Pediatric Dentistry

Pediatric dentistry is the branch of dentistry that treats children. The pediatric dentist will, therefore, be in charge of exploring and treating children and newborns. It is also responsible for detecting possible anomalies in the position of the jaws or teeth to refer to the orthodontist, specialist in orthodontics, and to make a restorative treatment if needed. The restorative treatment mainly consists of treating the traumatisms, using sealants that have the function of slightly filling the grooves and fissures of the teeth without hardly removing dental material to avoid possible decay, and in treating the caries produced and its consequences.
